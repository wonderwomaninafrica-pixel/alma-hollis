Alma Hollis writes emotionally immersive novels about the invisible ways love shapes us. Her stories explore grief in all its forms, not only the loss of people, but the loss of futures, identities, and the versions of ourselves we leave behind.

The Author

With luminous prose, quiet sensuality, and unforgettable emotional intimacy, Alma Hollis's novels remind readers that love is rarely measured by what we keep, but by what we are willing to carry.

A reader doesn't simply read an Alma Hollis novel. She feels it.
