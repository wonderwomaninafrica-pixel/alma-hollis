About Alma

I write about what haunts us, and what heals us.

My stories explore love in its rawest forms, family in all its complexity, and the truth we carry long after the page ends.

Alma Hollis writes emotionally immersive novels about the invisible ways love shapes us, exploring grief not only in the loss of people, but the loss of futures, identities, and the versions of ourselves we leave behind.
